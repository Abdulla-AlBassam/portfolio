## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and university admissions reviewing Abdulla AlBassam's cybersecurity portfolio. They arrive from LinkedIn, job applications, or direct links. They want to quickly assess technical competence, see real project work, and decide whether to shortlist or reach out.

### Brand Personality
**Technical, Clean, Confident.** The portfolio communicates professional engineering competence — precision without arrogance, skill without showing off.

### Emotional Goal
Visitors should feel **confident in Abdulla's skill** — the polish and professionalism of the site itself demonstrates the same attention to detail they'd expect in his technical work.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Dark terminal-inspired theme** (#0a0a0a background, #fafafa text, white accents — no colour)
- **Fonts**: Space Grotesk (headings), Inter (body)
- **Minimal and intentional** — every element earns its place
- **3D/shader effects** used sparingly for technical credibility, not decoration
- **Anti-references**: Generic WordPress portfolio templates, overly flashy neon/glassmorphism/AI-generated aesthetic. No glowing gradients, no gratuitous animation.

### Design Principles
1. **Substance over spectacle** — Technical craft should be evident but never gratuitous. The 3D elements and shaders demonstrate skill; they don't distract from content.
2. **Clarity first** — A recruiter scanning in 30 seconds should understand who Abdulla is, what he does, and how to reach him.
3. **Confident restraint** — Dark, clean, precise. Bold enough to stand out from template portfolios, restrained enough to feel professional.
4. **Accessibility matters** — WCAG AA compliance: proper contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader support, reduced motion respect.
5. **Performance is design** — Static generation, optimised images, selective hydration. Fast load times are part of the experience.

### Technical Stack
- Astro v4.16 (static site generator)
- Svelte v5 (interactive islands)
- Tailwind CSS v3.4 (utility-first styling)
- Three.js v0.170 + Threlte 8 (3D effects)
- TypeScript v5.6 (strict mode)
- Sharp (image optimisation)
- Vercel (deployment)
